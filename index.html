Current Features

SeedSense currently offers the following features:

  • Integrated GPS and mapping for trials
    This feature allows you to easily visualize the precise locations of all your trials on a map. It also helps you share directions with team members or collaborators, ensuring everyone knows exactly where each trial is located.
  • Thorough grading and evaluation options
    SeedSense provides versatile grading and evaluation options. You can write detailed evaluations in open text format or build custom templates tailored to specific crops. This flexibility ensures that you can accurately document and assess the performance of each variety.
  • Dynamic data management for locations, crops, suppliers, and varieties
    Our dynamic data management system helps you maintain comprehensive records of your organization's locations, crops, suppliers, and varieties. Easily access your team's trials and completed evaluations through intuitive and user-friendly catalogs, making data retrieval simple and efficient.
  • High quality image storage for evaluations
    Capture high-quality photos directly with the app or upload them at a later time. This feature allows you to visualize the progress and characteristics of each variety, providing a clear visual record to support your evaluations and decisions.
  • Task management for every user and trial
    Keep track of all the tasks involved in managing your trials, from planting and thinning to maturity checks and evaluations. Our task management feature ensures that every user knows their responsibilities and can stay on top of daily tasks without missing any critical steps.
  • Custom PDF trial maps and evaluation reports
    Once your trials are planted or evaluations are complete, you can create custom PDF trial maps and evaluation reports. These detailed documents allow you to share all the essential information about your trials with stakeholders, ensuring clear communication and comprehensive reporting.
