How It Works

Plant Your Trial

Log trial details like location, grower, crop variety, trial layout, and initial tasks to get started on the right foot.

Manage Tasks

Stay organized throughout the season by using tasks for thinning, monitoring, and collaborating with team members or farmers in the field.

Evaluate Your Trial

Use SeedSense to collect all evaluation data, including customized traits for each crop and high-quality images once the trial is mature.

Report and Share Data

Generate trial maps and evaluation reports to share via PDF or email, or access evaluations anywhere through the SeedSense cloud catalog.
